Our History


Started in 2020, Sheane Cosmetics quickly established itself as a shea butter products icon par excellence in Rwanda. With its unique brand of 100% natural products that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Rwanda. Featuring several recommendations and proven-results from customers in the Rwanda, you never know what will happen to your skin and hair when you choose our products.

Sheane is climate conscious, and takes every decision to help the environment and the healthy use of plastic to reduce waste.

Mardia Adams, Founder, Mardia K. Adams, 2022


Corporate Leadership


Mardia Adams - Founder

Our CEO, Mardia, credits her passion to the agricultural riches of her hometown, the leading producer of shea fruits and butter. Having stayed in the Northern region in Ghana and enjoyed the fun of going to gather shea fruits, was her first inspiration to create the Sheane Cosmetic Products. She seeks to produce natural cosmetic product from shea butter to glow the melanin skin of Africans
